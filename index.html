Latest projects

  • Chat Ergo Bot

    A powerful AI chatbot platform tailored for SMEs, with a web-to-chatbot converter, for chatbots that speak and listen. Chat Ergo Bot streamlines customer interactions and enhances user experience by leveraging NLP and machine learning technologies. The platform was built using Python, Flask among other technologies, and is designed to be easy to integrate and customize.

  • Quantum Algorithms through Machine Learning

    This innovative research project at the University of Murcia explored the potential of machine learning in analyzing and speeding up quantum algorithms. The project contributed to advancements in both quantum computing and machine learning, and resulted in two peer-reviewed publications that showcased the synergy between these two cutting-edge fields.

  • Super-Resolution Imaging in Neuroscience

    A collaborative project at ENS Paris - this interdisciplinary initiative focused on developing deep learning-based super-resolution imaging algorithms to improve the analysis of microscopic images in a neuroscience lab.
